Shortcut's

  - Sense:
    - V: Last Sense
    - R: Sense Target

 - Auto Stairs:
    - Space: Walks automatically to the stairs (you only need to press once time).

 - Auto Jump:
    - PageUp: Saves your current position for Jump Up.
    - PageDown: Saves your current position for Jump Down.
    - F: Automatically walk to the saved wall, turn in the correct direction and jump(if save positions is activated, just moving up/down will save the positions).

 - Push:
    - Shift + Directional(WASD QE ZC): Push the target to the pressed position.
    - R: When you press the key, it will save the position your mouse is in and will push the target to that position.
